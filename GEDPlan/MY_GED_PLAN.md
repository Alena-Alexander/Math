# GED Test
**`What is a GED`**:
A GED test is a High school equivalent credential that measures the knowledge and skills equivalent to a high school graduate.

**`What does one gain from passing?`**:
Passing the GED test allows for individuals to earn a certificate, or a degree that is generally accepted as equivalent to a high school diploma.
